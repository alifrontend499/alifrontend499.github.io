ABOUT FASHION ONE LLC

Fashion One is the foremost authority on global fashion, lifestyle and entertainment.

At Fashion One, our influence ranges from the latest news and trends from Fashion Weeks and Haute Couture to innovative, high-quality original programs, reality shows, documentaries, travelogues and lifestyle magazines.

From the exotica of Latin Angles, the telenovela-style drama of The Switch, the reality with a purpose of Fashion Correspondent, and the finger on the pulse of Eco Trendsetters and Fashion’s Future, Fashion One occupies rarified air in the entertainment industry.

Fashion One’s global office network spans from New York, Hong Kong, London, Portugal, Switzerland, Russia to Taiwan and the Philippines. Our continuing growth in North America, Eastern Europe, Africa and Asia has consolidated our position as the undisputed number one International fashion and lifestyle destination.

The network is a subsidiary of Bigfoot Entertainment launched in 2010.
