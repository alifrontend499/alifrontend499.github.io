1. If x4- y4 =15, where x and y are natural numbers, then find the value of the expression x4 + y4

keyboard_arrow_up
Correct ans is 17

Solution:

(x2 - y2)(x2 + y2) = 15

(x2 - y2)(x2 + y2) = 1x is 3 x 5. (x2 - y2) = 3 and (x2 + y2) = 5 (because x and y are natural numbers)

: 2 x 2

: 8 x 2 = 4

y2 = 1

x4 + y4 = 42 + 12 = 17
